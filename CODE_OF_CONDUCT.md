# Code of Conduct

## Purpose
Limelight‑X is a small, focused, and intentionally crafted open‑source project.  
Our goal is to create a space where contributors can collaborate with clarity, curiosity, and respect.  
This Code of Conduct outlines the expectations for everyone interacting with the project.

## Our Standards
We commit to a community that is:

- **Respectful** — We treat each other with consideration and kindness.  
- **Constructive** — We give helpful feedback and assume good intent.  
- **Clear** — We communicate with precision and avoid unnecessary ambiguity.  
- **Curious** — We ask questions, explore ideas, and welcome learning.  
- **Craft‑oriented** — We value quality, thoughtfulness, and care in contributions.

Examples of behavior that supports these values:

- Offering thoughtful, actionable feedback  
- Asking questions instead of making assumptions  
- Helping others understand the project’s design and philosophy  
- Being patient with newcomers  
- Keeping discussions focused and relevant  

## Unacceptable Behavior
The following behaviors are not allowed:

- Harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks  
- Demeaning or insulting comments  
- Disruptive or intentionally unproductive behavior  
- Publishing others’ private information without permission  
- Using the project to promote hate, violence, or illegal activity  
- Any conduct that violates GitHub’s Terms of Service  

## Responsibilities of Maintainers
Project maintainers are responsible for:

- Clarifying and enforcing this Code of Conduct  
- Reviewing and moderating contributions and discussions  
- Taking appropriate action when unacceptable behavior occurs  
- Communicating decisions respectfully and transparently  

Maintainers have the right to remove, edit, or reject contributions that do not align with this Code of Conduct or the project’s goals.

## Reporting Issues
If you experience or witness behavior that violates this Code of Conduct:

- Email the maintainers at: **don@donxml.com**  
- Or open a confidential issue labeled **conduct** (if appropriate)

Reports will be handled promptly, respectfully, and confidentially.

## Enforcement
Violations may result in actions including:

- A private warning  
- Temporary or permanent ban from contributing  
- Removal of inappropriate content  
- Escalation to GitHub support if necessary  

The maintainers’ decisions are final.

## Attribution
This Code of Conduct is adapted from the Contributor Covenant (v2.1) and tailored for the Limelight‑X project’s philosophy and scale.
